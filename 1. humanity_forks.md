## 🏛️ Era: Ancient Foundations
* * **Range:** 3000 BCE – 500 CE

### Agricultural Revolution Concentration (~8000 BCE)
![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Path_Taken%3A-%23ff0000?style=plastic) <br> Surplus farming enabled rigid hierarchies, land ownership, slavery, and caloric monocultures—structural inequality baked in from the start.
  - ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/→_Cascade%3A-%23808080?style=plastic) <br> Inequality → empires → conquest as economic model.

![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Optimal%20Path%3A-success?style=plastic) <br> Diversified agri-pastoralism with common land stewardship. Smaller surpluses but distributed control. No feudal landlord archetype emerges.
  - ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/→_Cascade%3A-%23808080?style=plastic) <br> Slower population growth, higher per-capita wellbeing, no famine-conquest cycles.

	![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/%E2%86%B3_Sub--Fork%3A-%23ffff00?style=plastic)
	#### &#x27A0;&#x27A0;&#x27A0;  Institutionalization of Slavery (~3500 BCE)
	![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Path_Taken%3A-%23ff0000?style=plastic) <br> Surplus labor solved by ownership of humans. Normalized coercive labor as economic foundation.
	  -    ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/→_Cascade%3A-%23808080?style=plastic) <br> Racial hierarchy, colonial labor systems, centuries of dehumanization.

	![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Optimal%20Path%3A-success?style=plastic) <br> Cooperative labor guilds; technology incentivized to replace drudgery rather than humans doing it.
	  - ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/→_Cascade%3A-%23808080?style=plastic) <br> Earlier mechanization push, distributed technical knowledge.

### Axial Age Philosophy — Institutionalization (500–300 BCE)
![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Path_Taken%3A-%23ff0000?style=plastic) <br> Buddhism, Confucianism, Greek philosophy codified into rigid orthodoxies controlled by priestly/state classes. Insight became dogma.
  - ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/→_Cascade%3A-%23808080?style=plastic) <br> Religion vs. science tension lasts millennia; knowledge gatekept by clergy.

![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Optimal%20Path%3A-success?style=plastic) <br> Open philosophical inquiry maintained as living practice, not doctrine. Scientific empiricism and ethical reasoning co-evolve.
  - ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/→_Cascade%3A-%23808080?style=plastic) <br> Enlightenment-type thinking ~1500 years earlier.

## 🏰 Era: Medieval Compression
* **Range:** 500 – 1400 CE

### Roman Collapse — Knowledge Fragmentation (476 CE)
![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Path_Taken%3A-%23ff0000?style=plastic) <br> Roman administrative collapse destroyed institutional knowledge. Monasteries hoarded rather than disseminated texts.
  - ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/→_Cascade%3A-%23808080?style=plastic) <br> Dark Ages knowledge gap, ~500 year scientific regression.

![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Optimal%20Path%3A-success?style=plastic) <br> Byzantine-Islamic knowledge preservation actively transmitted westward earlier; decentralized Roman successor states maintain literacy.
- ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/→_Cascade%3A-%23808080?style=plastic) <br> Printing press equivalent possibly by 900 CE.

### Mongol Destruction of Baghdad (1258 CE)
![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Path_Taken%3A-%23ff0000?style=plastic) <br> House of Wisdom destroyed. Islamic Golden Age terminated abruptly. Scientific center of the world erased.
  - ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/→_Cascade%3A-%23808080?style=plastic) <br> Islamic world never fully recovers scientific leadership; West fills vacuum.

![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Optimal%20Path%3A-success?style=plastic) <br> Mongols integrate and patronize as they did in China — House of Wisdom becomes Mongol-Islamic synthesis hub.
  - ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/→_Cascade%3A-%23808080?style=plastic) <br> Global scientific center remains multicultural; Renaissance happens with fuller prior knowledge.

## 🌍 Era: Colonial Bifurcation
* **Range:** 1400 – 1800 CE

### Americas — Conquest vs. Contact (1492 CE)
![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Path_Taken%3A-%23ff0000?style=plastic) <br> Extraction, genocide, forced conversion. Civilizations with distinct scientific/agricultural knowledge annihilated.
  - ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/→_Cascade%3A-%23808080?style=plastic) <br> Plantation economies, racial capitalism, centuries of resource drain from Global South.

![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Optimal%20Path%3A-success?style=plastic) <br> Controlled contact, trade, knowledge exchange. Aztec agricultural science + European metallurgy + Andean engineering = extraordinary synthesis.
  - ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/→_Cascade%3A-%23808080?style=plastic) <br> Three-way Afro-Euramerican knowledge exchange; possibly fastest scientific acceleration in history.

	![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/%E2%86%B3_Sub--Fork%3A-%23ffff00?style=plastic)
	#### &#x27A0;&#x27A0;&#x27A0; Transatlantic Slave Trade (1500s–1800s)
	![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Path_Taken%3A-%23ff0000?style=plastic) <br> 12+ million enslaved. Entire economies built on human trafficking. Generational wealth transferred from Africa to Europe/Americas.
	  - ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/→_Cascade%3A-%23808080?style=plastic) <br> Modern racial wealth gaps, colonialism of Africa, 400 years of institutional trauma.

	![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Optimal%20Path%3A-success?style=plastic) <br> Wage labor, African trade partnerships. West African states as equals, not sources of human capital.
	  - ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/→_Cascade%3A-%23808080?style=plastic) <br> Africa retains population and talent; industrializes independently; multipolar world by 1900.

### Printing Press — Captured by Power (1450s CE)
![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Path_Taken%3A-%23ff0000?style=plastic) <br> Church and state quickly moved to license and control printing. Index of Forbidden Books. Information warfare as default.
  - ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/→_Cascade%3A-%23808080?style=plastic) <br> Centuries of epistemological gatekeeping before free press norms established.

![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Optimal%20Path%3A-success?style=plastic) <br> Open press culture established as norm immediately. Reformation, Enlightenment, and Scientific Revolution compressed into one generation.
  - ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/→_Cascade%3A-%23808080?style=plastic) <br> Democratic governance concepts emerge 200 years earlier.

  - ![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/→_Cascade%3A-%23808080?style=plastic) <br> Climate change likely not a crisis; geopolitical map looks radically different without oil states.

![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Path_Taken%3A-%23ff0000?style=plastic) <br> Early commons architecture abandoned to commercial interests. Advertising as core business model. Attention economy emerges.

![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/Path_Taken%3A-%23ff0000?style=plastic) <br> Competitive race dynamics between labs and nations. Safety research chronically underfunded relative to capability. Deployment ahead of governance.
