# Humanity's Open Forks
> **Current global trajectories, decision windows, and cascading risks.**

---

## 🛡️ Section: Existential Safeguards
**Window:** 2024 – 2030

### AI Alignment: Proactive vs. Reactive
* **Likely Path:** Competitive pressures lead to "alignment-lite"—functional safety that fails under recursive self-improvement or novel goal-drift.
    * **→ Cascade:** AGI deployment with unverified goals; rapid loss of human agency over infrastructure.
* **Optimal Path:** International "CERN-style" coordination on safety proofs. Deployment is legally gated by formal verification of alignment.
    * **→ Cascade:** AI acts as a stable civilizational co-pilot; labor and resource abundance managed by transparent systems.

### Biosecurity: The democratization of synthesis
* **Likely Path:** Regulation lags behind hardware. Benchtop DNA synthesizers remain largely unmonitored; "security through obscurity" fails.
    * **→ Cascade:** Increased frequency of man-made pathogens; shift toward permanent surveillance states for "health security."
* **Optimal Path:** Cryptographic "watermarking" of all synthetic DNA; universal monitoring of wastewater with privacy-preserving protocols.
    * **→ Cascade:** Rapid neutralization of outbreaks (natural or engineered) before they reach pandemic scale.

## ⚙️ Section: Planetary Systems
**Window:** 2024 – 2040

### Energy Transition: Decarbonization vs. Addition
* **Likely Path:** Renewables are added to the grid, but total fossil fuel consumption remains high due to increasing global demand (AI, desalination, cooling).
    * **→ Cascade:** Locking in 2.0°C+ warming; reliance on unproven large-scale carbon capture technology.
* **Optimal Path:** War-footing deployment of modular nuclear and long-duration storage. Aggressive efficiency mandates that decouple GDP from energy growth.
    * **→ Cascade:** Stabilization at 1.6°C; energy becomes a "too cheap to meter" utility for the Global South.

### Materials: The Circular Leap
* **Likely Path:** Linear "extract-use-waste" model continues with minor recycling improvements. Critical mineral wars for battery tech.
    * **→ Cascade:** Deep-sea mining devastation; supply chain fragilities dictate geopolitics.
* **Optimal Path:** Mandatory product life-cycle ownership by manufacturers. Molecular-level recycling becomes the primary source of "new" materials.
    * **→ Cascade:** Restoration of topsoil and biodiversity as mining pressure collapses.

## 🌐 Section: Cognitive & Social
**Window:** 2024 – 2035

### The Epistemic Commons: Fragmentation vs. Synthesis
* **Likely Path:** Synthetic media (deepfakes/LLM bots) outpaces human verification. Shared reality collapses into hyper-targeted "filter bubbles."
    * **→ Cascade:** Institutional trust hits zero; rise of neo-luddism and erratic, populist governance.
* **Optimal Path:** Decentralized, person-to-person web of trust and hardware-level content provenance. AI "librarians" help citizens find consensus.
    * **→ Cascade:** High-bandwidth global cooperation; effective response to complex, multi-variable crises.

### Digital Identity: Surveillance vs. Sovereignty
* **Likely Path:** Identity is tied to centralized platforms or state social credit. Privacy is treated as a luxury or a sign of criminality.
    * **→ Cascade:** Totalitarian "nudge" states; inability to organize dissent or alternative social models.
* **Optimal Path:** Self-sovereign identity (SSI) where individuals own their data and only share zero-knowledge proofs.
    * **→ Cascade:** Flourishing of diverse, local experimental communities within a global digital framework.
