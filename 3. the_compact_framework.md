# THE COMPACT
### A Framework for Planetary Governance of Existential-Scale Risks

**Working Document** · **Classification:** Open · **Version:** 0.1 · **Date:** February 2026

---

## 1. Preamble — Honest Starting Point
> **Warning:** This document does not assume good faith, shared values, or human altruism as inputs. It is engineered for a world where states lie, corporations optimise for profit, and individuals act in short-term self-interest. Any framework that requires trust to function will fail. This one is designed to function because defection is made more expensive than compliance — for everyone, including the designers.

* **The Problem:** The core problem with every existing international governance mechanism — the UN, WTO, ICJ, WHO — is the same: they operate on state consent and have no enforcement teeth against permanent members of the Security Council or economically indispensable actors.
* **The Reality:** Russia ignores ECHR rulings. The US refuses to ratify the Law of the Sea while criticising China for violating it. The ICC has indicted 44 Africans and one Russian in 23 years.
* **The Design:** This is a ground-up redesign that treats governance as an engineering problem, not a diplomacy problem. What is the minimum viable architecture that constrains all actors — including the most powerful — without requiring them to trust each other?
* **The Answer:** Verified consequences, distributed enforcement, and structuring compliance as the economically rational choice. This is how MAD worked during the Cold War. This is how SWIFT works now.

## 2. Design Principles

### What this framework explicitly is NOT
Not a world government. Nations retain full sovereignty over domestic matters. The Compact has jurisdiction only over **planetary-commons risks** — threats whose consequences cannot be contained within any single border. A country's internal tax policy is none of the Compact's business. A country's AI weapons programme or engineered pathogen research is.

### Core Engineering Constraints
* **No single point of control:** No nation, corporation, or individual can unilaterally control the framework. All enforcement decisions require multi-body consensus with transparent override logs.
* **Self-funding and self-enforcing:** Revenue comes from mandatory levies on the activities it regulates: compute sales, pharmaceutical synthesis, weapons-grade material transactions.
* **Transparent by default:** All decisions, deliberations, and evidence submitted are public within 90 days. Opacity is the mechanism by which governance is captured.
* **Adversarially designed:** Every mechanism has been stress-tested against the question: "how would a bad actor — a powerful state, a rogue corporation, a captured regulator — exploit this?"

## 3. Architecture — Five Pillars

* **Pillar 01: The Registry**
    Mandatory global registration of: frontier AI training runs (>10²³ FLOPs), DNA synthesis above defined thresholds, weapons-grade fissile material, space launch manifests, and pandemic-class pathogen research. No registration = automatic sanctions trigger.
* **Pillar 02: The Council**
    45-member deliberative body. **No veto power.** Seats: 15 elected by global population (democratic), 15 by nation-state agreement (geopolitical), 15 by scientific/civil society lottery from a verified expert pool (epistemic). 3-year non-renewable terms.
* **Pillar 03: The Tribunal**
    Independent judiciary — judges selected by algorithm from pre-verified pool with conflict-of-interest screening removing judges with national ties to the case. No political appointment pathway.
* **Pillar 04: The Enforcement Arm**
    Primary leverage is economic and technical: access to the global financial clearing system (SWIFT), semiconductor supply chains, space launch infrastructure, and pharmaceutical patent protections are all Compact-gated.
* **Pillar 05: The Transparency Engine**
    Public-facing real-time dashboard of all Registry entries, Council votes, Tribunal proceedings, and enforcement actions. Powered by redundant distributed infrastructure — no single state can take it down.

## 4. Enforcement — Violation Tiers

| Tier | Trigger | Consequence | Severity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | Registry Failure | Automatic public disclosure + 90-day compliance window + financial surcharge | Administrative |
| **2** | Compliance Breach | Activity suspended pending audit; access to Compact-gated trade benefits suspended for that domain | Operational |
| **3** | Material Violation | Full economic sanctions package (SWIFT access, semiconductor exports, patent protections suspended) | Economic |
| **4** | Existential Breach | Full isolation protocol: all Compact-gated infrastructure withdrawn; collective defensive response authorised | Existential |

## 5. Attack Surfaces — Adversarial Analysis
*Verbatim data from the internal simulation arrays:*

### Regulatory Capture
* **How it's exploited:** Corporations place former employees or funded academics in Council seats. Regulations written to disadvantage competitors. Definition of "safe" AI gradually shifts to match what leading labs already produce.
* **Mitigation:** Industry representatives barred from roles. 10-year cooling-off period. All deliberations public within 90 days. Independent audit of financial interests published quarterly.

### Great Power Veto via Funding Withdrawal
* **How it's exploited:** Dominant economies (US, China, EU) threaten to withhold funding unless rules are written to their advantage. This is how the US has historically constrained UN action.
* **Mitigation:** Compact funded by mandatory levies on regulated activities (GPU sales, DNA synthesis) — not voluntary contributions. No single state contributes more than 15% of budget.

### Definition Creep
* **How it's exploited:** What counts as a "frontier AI system" or "weapons-grade pathogen" is gradually redefined to exempt current activities. The framework remains nominally intact while covering nothing dangerous.
* **Mitigation:** Technical definitions locked in foundational treaty and amendable only by supermajority. Separate "technical standards board" of scientists sets quantitative thresholds insulated from the political body.

### Emergency Powers Abuse
* **How it's exploited:** A genuine crisis is used to invoke emergency powers that never sunset. "Temporary" Compact authority over member state domestic policy becomes permanent.
* **Mitigation:** Emergency Council powers automatically expire after 90 days unless re-ratified by full Council. Emergency measures cannot override Tribunal jurisdiction.

## 6. The AI's Single Most Important Recommendation
**Pre-draft the treaty text now, in public.** When the crisis arrives — and it will — the political window for action is measured in weeks, not years. Governance assembled from scratch under pressure produces the UN: structurally compromised from birth. The design work is the most valuable thing that can be done before the crisis. After it, there is no time.
