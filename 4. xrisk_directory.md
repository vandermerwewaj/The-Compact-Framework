## 🏛️ Policy & Governance Organizations
*These groups focus on the "Compact" style of structural and legal frameworks.*

### 1. Centre for the Study of Existential Risk (CSER)
* **Abbreviation:** CSER
* **Location:** 📍 Cambridge, UK
* **Focus:** AI, Bio, Climate, Nuclear
* **Description:** An interdisciplinary research centre focused on the management of extreme risks and the long-term future of humanity.
* **Engagement:**
    * **Research:** Technical papers on volcanic risk and AI alignment.
    * **Policy:** Advising the UK government on national risk registers.
* **URL:** ↗ cser.ac.uk

### 2. Future of Humanity Institute (FHI)
* **Abbreviation:** FHI
* **Location:** 📍 Oxford, UK
* **Focus:** AI, Bio, Macrostrategy
* **Description:** (Historical Note: Founded by Nick Bostrom). Focused on big-picture questions for humanity using mathematics, philosophy, and social sciences.
* **Engagement:**
    * **Foundational:** Produced the seminal works on Astronomical Waste and Singleton theory.
* **URL:** ↗ fhi.ox.ac.uk

### 3. Ada Lovelace Institute
* **Location:** 📍 London, UK
* **Focus:** AI, Data Ethics
* **Description:** Ensuring data and AI work for people and society. Focuses heavily on the "Brussels Effect" of regulation.
* **Engagement:**
    * **Policy:** Deep engagement with the EU AI Act and UK AI regulation white papers.
* **URL:** ↗ adalovelaceinstitute.org

## 🤖 AI Safety & Alignment Labs
*Technical groups working on the "Pillar 01" Registry and verification challenges.*

### 1. Machine Intelligence Research Institute (MIRI)
* **Abbreviation:** MIRI
* **Location:** 📍 Berkeley, USA
* **Focus:** AGI Alignment
* **Description:** One of the oldest organizations in the space. Known for a "pessimistic" but rigorous mathematical approach to the alignment problem.
* **Engagement:**
    * **Technical:** Research into logical induction and agent foundations.
* **URL:** ↗ intelligence.org

### 2. Alignment Research Center (ARC)
* **Abbreviation:** ARC
* **Location:** 📍 Berkeley, USA
* **Focus:** AI Eliciting Latent Knowledge (ELK)
* **Description:** Founded by Paul Christiano. Focuses on the "Evaluation" side of the Compact—testing if models are being "honest" with their designers.
* **Engagement:**
    * **Testing:** Providing "Red Teaming" for major labs like OpenAI and Anthropic.
* **URL:** ↗ alignment.org

### 3. Center for AI Safety (CAIS)
* **Abbreviation:** CAIS
* **Location:** 📍 San Francisco, USA
* **Focus:** AI Risk Communication
* **Description:** Organized the "Statement on AI Risk" signed by top industry leaders. Focuses on making AI risk a mainstream policy priority.
* **URL:** ↗ safe.ai
